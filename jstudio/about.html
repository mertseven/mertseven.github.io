About

Showcasing journalism projects by third-year New Media and Communication students at Yaşar University.

Our Program

The Journalism Studio course is designed to prepare future journalists for the evolving media landscape. Through hands-on projects and innovative storytelling techniques, students develop the skills needed to excel in modern journalism.

Student Projects

Each semester, our students produce in-depth journalism projects that combine traditional reporting with new media technologies. These projects represent the culmination of their learning journey and showcase their ability to tell compelling stories across multiple platforms.

Our Approach

We believe in learning by doing. Our curriculum emphasizes practical experience, critical thinking, and innovative storytelling techniques. Students work with industry-standard tools and technologies while maintaining the core principles of ethical journalism.
