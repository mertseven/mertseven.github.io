ABOUT ME

I'm a sociologist [PhD] who loves exploring new technologies, design, and ideas of the time. My work sits at the intersection of digital culture and human behavior, where I investigate how technology shapes our ways of knowing and being. With a foundation in sociology and a passion for new media, I approach technology not just as tools to use, but as phenomena that transform how we think, connect, and create meaning in the digital age.

This website functions as a digital playground, continuously evolving and integrating new aspects of web design and interaction. It draws inspiration from Hegel's philosophical concept of "becoming" - a fundamental idea about how things exist and change.

ON BECOMING

Hegel proposed that nothing simply "is" in a fixed state. Instead, everything exists in a process of becoming something else. Think of how a seed becomes a plant - it's neither just a seed nor just a plant, but rather exists in a continuous process of development. Similarly, this website isn't a finished product but a space in constant transformation, where new features emerge and existing ones evolve.

X-RAY VIEWER

MOONBASE TERMINAL

An interactive command-line interface that provides another way to navigate and interact with the site. Type 'help' to discover available commands. This feature reimagines website navigation through a more technical, hands-on approach.

Each visit might reveal something new, as features are added and refined. The site exists not as a finished product but as an ongoing experiment in web development and design, always in the process of becoming something new.
