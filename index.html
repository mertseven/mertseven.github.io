The Philosophy: Ever Becoming

Hegel proposed that nothing simply "is" in a fixed state. Instead, everything exists in a process of becoming something else. Think of how a seed becomes a plant - it's neither just a seed nor just a plant, but rather exists in a continuous process of development. Similarly, this website isn't a finished product but a space in constant transformation, where new features emerge and existing ones evolve.

Collaborating with tools like Anthropic's Claude AI and Google's Gemini AI is integral to this process, allowing for deeper investigations into human-AI collaboration and the creation of more insightful, reflective digital artifacts.
