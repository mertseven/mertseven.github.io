Mert Seven, PhD

I am a new media scholar and digital crafter. I design and build interactive systems as a form of critical inquiry—turning abstract theories of digital culture into tangible, explorable experiences.

My work, including this website, is treated as a living artifact. It is a space for investigating how technology shapes our perception and knowledge. Grounded in sociology and a PhD in Communication Research, I approach new media not as a set of tools, but as a system of logic to be both used and questioned. I am always open to discussing new projects, research collaborations, or speaking opportunities.
