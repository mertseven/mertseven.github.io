Projects

My projects weave together digital media, interactive tools, and educational experiments—each one a curious exploration at the intersection of technology, design, and social understanding. I build these digital playgrounds to investigate how humans and technology interact, creating systems that reveal patterns in our collective experiences and behaviors.

These works aren't just tools or websites; they're windows into how we communicate, learn, and connect in the digital age. They reflect my fascination with making complex concepts tangible through interactive experiences.
